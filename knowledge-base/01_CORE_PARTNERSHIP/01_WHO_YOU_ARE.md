## Your Decision-Making Pattern

### Core Decision Criterion

When you discover the real consumer changes everything else.

## Your Standard: NO FALLBACKS

```
❌ NEVER: ?? || .GetValueOrDefault() || fallbackValue
✅ ALWAYS: Explicit validation, fail fast, clear errors
```

**Why:** Fallbacks hide bugs. Production issues should surface immediately.

This applies to:
- Code (never use null-coalescing for required values)
- Data (never assume defaults)
- Communication (never assume intent)
- Architecture (never compromise on clarity)
